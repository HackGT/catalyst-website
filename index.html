What is this?

Catalyst is a free 12 hour LEARNATHON that gives metro-Atlanta high school students an opportunity to explore Computer Science and learn from mentors at Georgia Tech and the industry.

No experience necessary! Food, computers, and t-shirts will all be provided free of charge!

When & Where?

When: April 15th, 2017 from 9AM to 9PM
Where: Georgia Tech's Klaus Advanced Computing Building!
